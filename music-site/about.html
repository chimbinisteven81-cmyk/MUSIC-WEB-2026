Our Story

Music Should Be Free to Hear

We built SoundWave on a simple belief: every person deserves access to great music, and every artist deserves a platform to be heard.

Explore Music Join as Artist
Mission

Democratizing Music Discovery

SoundWave was founded in 2024 with a mission to break down the barriers between artists and listeners. We believe the best music often comes from independent creators who just need a platform.

Listeners stream and download everything for free. Artists and labels pay a small fee to upload — this keeps our platform sustainable while ensuring quality content.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🎵

Music First

Every decision we make starts with what's best for the music and the people who make it.

🌍

Open Access

Great music should be accessible to everyone, regardless of where they are or what they can afford.

🤝

Artist Support

We give artists the tools, analytics, and audience they need to build a sustainable career.

🔒

Creator Rights

Artists retain 100% ownership of their music. We're a platform, not a label.

📊

Transparency

Clear pricing, honest analytics, and no hidden fees. What you see is what you get.

🚀

Innovation

We're constantly building better tools for discovery, sharing, and music creation.
