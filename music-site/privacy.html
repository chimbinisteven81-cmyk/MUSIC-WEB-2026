At SMADS African Hits, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

SMADS African Hits is an African music streaming and distribution platform based in Zambia, operated by Chimbini Steven. We can be reached at support@smadsafricanhits.com.

2. What We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account data: username, email address, display name, password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Profile data: bio, website, profile photo, country
  • Music uploads: audio files, cover art, track metadata (title, genre, tags)
  • Payment data: plan selected, payment method type, transaction reference. We do not store card numbers — all payments are processed by our payment partner.
  • Messages: content you submit via the contact form

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: tracks played, downloads, likes, playlist activity
  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system (for security and analytics)
  • Login history: time, IP address, and device of each login attempt

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Platform (account management, streaming, uploads)
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password reset, payment receipts)
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Improve the Platform through analytics (play counts, download trends, genre popularity)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Public profile: your display name, profile photo, uploaded tracks, and follower count are publicly visible
  • Payment processors: we share necessary transaction data with our payment partner to process uploads
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose data if required by Zambian law or a valid court order
  • Business transfer: if the Platform is acquired or merged, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use:

  • Session cookies: to keep you logged in during your visit. These expire when you close your browser or log out.
  • localStorage: to save your notification preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

6. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing — never in plain text
  • All API requests use CSRF token validation
  • Login attempts are rate-limited to prevent brute force attacks
  • Session cookies are HTTP-only and cannot be accessed by JavaScript
  • File uploads are validated for type and content before being accepted

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to legal@smadsafricanhits.com.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days
  • Uploaded tracks are removed immediately upon account deletion
  • Payment records are retained for 7 years for financial compliance
  • Login history is retained for 90 days then automatically deleted

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: update your profile information at any time in your account settings
  • Deletion: delete your account and all associated data from your profile settings
  • Portability: request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@smadsafricanhits.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

SMADS African Hits is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify registered users by email and update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: support@smadsafricanhits.com
  • Legal inquiries: legal@smadsafricanhits.com
  • Contact form: smadsafricanhits.com/contact
