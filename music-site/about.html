Our Story

Music Should Be Free to Hear

We built SMADS African Hits on a simple belief: every person deserves access to great music, and every artist deserves a platform to be heard.

Explore Music
Mission

Democratizing Music Discovery

SMADS African Hits was founded in 2024 with a mission to break down the barriers between artists and listeners. We believe the best music often comes from independent creators who just need a platform.

Listeners stream and download everything for free. Artists and labels pay a small fee to upload — this keeps our platform sustainable while ensuring quality content.

Our Values

🎵

Music First

Every decision we make starts with what's best for the music and the people who make it.

🌎

Open Access

Great music should be accessible to everyone, regardless of where they are or what they can afford.

Artist Support

Journey

Our Story So Far

💡
2024 — Q1

The Idea

Chimbini Steven identifies the gap: African artists have no dedicated platform to distribute music and grow with real data.

💡
2024 — Q3

Development Begins

SMADS African Hits is built from the ground up — free downloads for listeners, paid uploads for artists, ZMW pricing.

💡
2025 — Q1

Zambia Launch

Platform launches in Zambia with local artists. MTN and Airtel Money payment support added.

💡
2026 — Now

African Expansion

Growing across Africa. Charts, analytics, and artist verification system live. The African music economy is being built.
